We love art and we care...

We are KUUA, an indie gallery with the mission of building bridges of communication through art. We are passionate about colors and bold messages and we believe in the power of images to spread ideas.

We create and exhibit pieces of pop art with sometimes twisted and funny messages that would hopefully spark real conversations. Our work is in hands of private collectors, offices, restaurants and hotels in United Arab Emirates, The Netherlands, Lebanon, KSA and Argentina.

KUUA creates and displays big size original art pieces made with acrylivcs, oils, ink and prints to decorate and to hopefully challenge the viewer to look beneath the surface of the canvas.

We also want to help so we decided to add our modest bit: Half of our net profits are donated to the VUmc to help them take care of people.
