KUUA

We love art and we care...

We are KUUA, an indie gallery with the mission of spreading love through art. We believe in the power of images to spread ideas.

We create and exhibit pieces of pop art with sometimes twisted and funny messages that would hopefully spark real conversations. Our work is in hands of private collectors, offices, restaurants and hotels in United Arab Emirates, The Netherlands, Lebanon, KSA, Argentina and more.

We also want to help so we decided to add our very small bit: Half our profits are donated to the VUmc to help them take care of people. Do you want to own today a piece that will stay with your family for generations? get in contact!
